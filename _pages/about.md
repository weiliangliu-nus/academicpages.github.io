<h2>Research Interests</h2>
<p> My research combines stochastic modeling, optimization, data analytics, and statistics to address emerging challenges in modern service operations by bridging theory and practice to generate actionable insights, with applications spanning public and private sectors such as medical emergency response, drone delivery, on-demand staffing, and online matching platforms.
</p>

<p> 
I am also passionate about advancing theories at the intersection of queuing control and data-driven decision-making, where unknown primitives must be inferred from data.</p>
